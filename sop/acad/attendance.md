## Purpose 
The purpose of this Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is to establish a uniform framework for _monitoring and regulating student attendance, leave, medical leave, and Official Duty (OD) requirements_ at TIFAC-CORE in Cyber Security in accordance with University Policy.

## Standard Guidelines
### 1. Minimum Requirement

1.1 Students must maintain **100% attendance**. <br/> 
1.2 A **minimum of 75% attendance in every subject** is mandatory to be eligible to appear for **end-semester examinations**.

### 2. Leave Rules

2.1 Leave may be availed only under **unavoidable circumstances**. <br/> 
2.2 Students must **apply for leave in the prescribed form before proceeding on leave**. <br/> 
2.3 The leave application must be **recommended by the Class Advisor/Counsellor** and submitted to the **Director of the Centre** for consideration and approval through Department Secretary. <br/> 
2.4 **Unauthorized absence** will be treated as **disciplinary misconduct**. <br/> 
2.5 For **non-medical leave exceeding three consecutive days**, the application must be **countersigned by the parent/guardian or the Warden**, as applicable. 

### 3. Medical Leave

3.1 For **medical leave exceeding three consecutive days**, a **medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner (with Registration Number and Seal)** must be submitted, along with supporting documents such as **in-patient records**. <br/> 
3.2 The medical leave application must be submitted to the **Class Advisor/Counsellor on the day of return after the period of absence**. <br/> 
3.3 Medical leave **does not automatically grant attendance exemption**. The **minimum 75% attendance rule applies unless specifically condoned**.

### 4. Official Duty (OD) Leave

Students are eligible for **Official Duty (OD) Leave** when participating in the following: <br/> 
4.1 **National / International Technical Competitions** including Ideathon, Hackathon, Pentathon, Startup Competitions. <br/> 
4.2 **Sports and Cultural events**, including those conducted within the University, and institution-wide activities such as **Gokulashtami, Amritotsavam**, etc. <br/> 
4.3 **Club Activities officially recognized by the Office of Students Affiars (OSA)** <br/> 
4.4 **Seminars and Conferences** prescribed by the University or those approved in advance by the **Director – TIFAC-CORE in Cyber Security** <br/> 
4.5 **Training, Workshops, or Participation** related to the above activities 

### 5. Conditions for OD Leave

5.1 **OD leave must be applied for in advance before attending the event.** <br/> 
5.2 Students must submit **valid proof/certificates of participation** for OD approval and for attendance to be updated in the **MyAmrita portal**. <br/> 
5.3 For **Internships arranged by students on their own**, **prior approval from the Director – TIFAC-CORE is mandatory**. The **Internship Completion Certificate** must be submitted at the end of the internship. <br/> 
5.4 **Only 8 ODs per course per semester are permitted**, as per the circular from the **Dean – Academics**. <br/> 

> [!Note]
> - The **OD form must be submitted before the event**; however, it will be **sanctioned only after submission of participation proof**.

> [!Warning]
> - **Late submission of Leave or OD Form beyond the notified time limit will not be entertained**.
